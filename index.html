Welcome to MSRIT

Ramaiah Institute of Technology (RIT) is one of India’s best-known engineering education institute founded in 1962 by renowned industrialist, philanthropist and visionary the Late Dr. M.S. Ramaiah. RIT has received the prestigious Autonomous Status in the year 2007. RIT Ranked among top 100 Institutions across India for the last four years, as per National Institute Ranking Frame Work (NIRF), MHRD, Govt. of India.
